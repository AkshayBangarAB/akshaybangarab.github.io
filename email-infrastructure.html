Frequently Asked Questions

Email Infrastructure FAQ

Common questions about cold email infrastructure, SMTP setup, and deliverability.

What is cold email infrastructure?

Cold email infrastructure is a complete system for sending outreach emails at scale. It includes dedicated servers, SMTP software like PowerMTA, email management platforms like MailWizz, proper DNS and authentication setup (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), IP warm-up, and deliverability monitoring to ensure emails land in the inbox rather than the spam folder.

What services do you offer for email infrastructure?

I provide end-to-end email infrastructure services including SMTP server deployment, PowerMTA & MailWizz configuration, domain and DNS setup, email authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC, rDNS), IP and domain warm-up, email verification, deliverability optimization, campaign setup, and ongoing monitoring. Every infrastructure is custom-built based on your sending requirements.

Why is email authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC) important?

Email authentication protocols like SPF, DKIM, and DMARC verify that emails are sent from authorized servers, prevent spoofing, and build sender reputation. Proper authentication is critical for inbox placement, avoiding spam filters, and maintaining a strong sender reputation for cold email campaigns.

What is PowerMTA and why is it used for cold email?

PowerMTA (PMTA) is an enterprise-grade SMTP server software designed for high-volume email delivery. It supports virtual MTAs, IP rotation, queue management, and advanced throttling — making it ideal for cold email infrastructure where deliverability, scalability, and sender reputation management are critical.

How does IP warm-up improve email deliverability?

IP warm-up is the process of gradually increasing email sending volume on a new IP address to build a positive sender reputation with mailbox providers like Gmail, Outlook, and Yahoo. It prevents emails from being flagged as spam and helps achieve consistent inbox placement over time.

Do you provide ongoing email infrastructure management?

Yes. Beyond initial setup, I offer ongoing server monitoring, deliverability audits, blacklist resolution, reputation management, campaign performance optimization, and infrastructure scaling as your sending volume grows.

Is cold email legal? How do you ensure compliance?

Yes, B2B cold email outreach is legal when done in compliance with applicable regulations. All infrastructure setups I build include proper unsubscribe mechanisms, accurate sender identification, and follow best practices aligned with CAN-SPAM (US), GDPR (EU), and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023. I focus exclusively on legitimate B2B outreach — not spam.
